# ACIS Generation One — Research Agenda

> Produced by ACIS Generation Zero (Fable 5, 2026-07-11). These are E0 prompts — questions whose
> answers could *invalidate* ACIS, ranked by how much of the theory they would take down. They are
> deliberately **unresolved**: a model resolving its own invalidators is not independent evidence
> (`T-ACIS-MODEL-CONSENSUS-001`). Full law records: `vault/knowledge_base/acis/acis_01_generation_zero_laws.md`.

| # | Threatens | Question | Falsifying test |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | LAW-07 (highest) | Is epistemic level *actually* reliably derivable from disk artifacts, or are there capabilities whose real status no artifact captures? | Audit a deposit sample for human-judged vs derived-level disagreement |
| 2 | the PLAN verdict | Does making level explicit change behavior, or is it inert documentation? | Measure whether the derived level ever gates a real done-claim |
| 3 | LAW-05 | Can saturation be gamed — declaring saturation to bank budget while a real delta remained? | Re-open a "saturated" bounded domain with a fresh model; check for above-floor deltas |
| 4 | LAW-03 / LAW-08 | Is the frontier/deterministic boundary stable, or does model improvement continuously move it? | Track capability boundary crossings over model versions; add a time-decay term to LAW-08 if fast |
| 5 | `T-ACIS-MODEL-CONSENSUS-001` | Is a *different model* genuine independent evidence, or is model diversity illusory? | Compare Opus re-derivation agreement against production-evidence agreement |
| 6 | ACIS itself (meta) | Is a self-evolving law system net-positive, or does law/level/falsifier upkeep exceed the compounding it produces? | Measure ACIS maintenance cost vs dependence-curve movement over a quarter |

**Escalation criterion:** open a multi-session ACIS campaign only if ≥3 of these return ADMIT
after a preflight (the floor does not already answer them). Untested today — the agenda is the
deliverable, not its resolution. Route each via `session_compiler` preflight before spending
frontier tokens; most may route ROUTE_CHEAPER, which is the FD-00 gate working.
