The source is a brainstorm that proposes ~30 guardian agents, 5 fractal "civilizations", a
19-macrofamily meta-governance kernel, a Cognitive Capital OS (CCOS), and a Cognitive Reasoning
Compilation Fabric (CRCF). Measured against the disk: **the Power Pack already ships ~22 of the
30 founding guardians, and CCOS is substantially owned by the Cognitive OS (CO-00…CO-12).** What
does *not* exist anywhere in the estate is the substrate the source itself identifies as the real
unlock — **typed cognitive intermediate representations, a fidelity/loss contract, and a formal
Obligation IR.** The corpus therefore is **not 5 civilizations and 500,000 words**. It is a
**compilation substrate: 8 datasets, spearheaded by 4.**

The source anticipates this conclusion itself, twice: *"esas capacidades siguen siendo islas"*, and
*"El Unfinished Work Watcher deja de buscar texto suelto. Consume una IR formal."* The IR is the gap.
Everything else is an island that already exists.
