## 1. The compounding thesis

The corpus's value is not the sum of eight datasets. It is the loop they close, which no dataset closes alone:

```
DAIF-00  governs what may be built at all      (reinforce before create)
   ↓
DAIF-01  gives knowledge a common vocabulary   (typed units: what may be checked)
   ↓
DAIF-02  compiles it into representations      (CIR-0…11: what may be reasoned over)
   ↓
DAIF-03  proves the compilation was faithful   (or REFUSES: what may be trusted)
   ↓
DAIF-08  delivers it against a mission         (page faults: what may be corrected mid-flight)
   ↓
DAIF-07  tracks what remains undone            (obligations: what may not vanish)
   ↓
DAIF-04  types the promises between them all   (contracts: what may be breached, not merely damaged)
   ↓
DAIF-21  detects that reality moved            (invalidation: what may not silently rot)
   ↓
   └──────→ back to DAIF-02 (recompile) + DAIF-07 (a tracked obligation to do it)
```

**The seven silent failures the loop defends against — one per dataset:**

| Without | The system… |
|---|---|
| DAIF-01 types | cannot check its own knowledge; every rule is a sentence someone must remember to read |
| DAIF-02 compilation | re-reasons forever; every session starts from zero |
| DAIF-03 fidelity | has only compressed *hopefully*; a dropped hard constraint is indistinguishable from a good summary |
| DAIF-07 obligations | finishes nothing it starts; vanished work raises no error |
| DAIF-04 contracts | learns of its promises only through their damage |
| DAIF-08 runtime | works confidently from quiet omissions; the agent guesses instead of asking |
| DAIF-21 sync | becomes a museum of confident falsehoods — every exhibit accurate on the day it was mounted |

## 5. Honest residuals (the corpus's own, restated here — never hidden)

1. **DAIF is a specification.** No compiler runs. No page fault has ever fired. No obligation has been extracted from a real conversation. No false-VALID rate has been sampled — there is nothing yet to sample.
2. **Four claims are unproven:** that compiled packs preserve behavior; that CIR-5 extraction actually captures the offhand-aside obligation; that the recompilation economy stays bounded; that semantic-change detection catches enough to matter.
3. **The adoption risk is real:** 292k words is a large corpus, and a governance regime heavier than the failures it prevents gets routed around — which the corpus itself convicts as a failure mode.
4. **The first proving vertical** (session continuity, DAIF-08 Part XI) exists precisely so that the **first claim made about DAIF is a measured one**. Its done-gate: 100% of hard constraints and 100% of open obligations survive a context reset; state correctly identified; no indiscriminate re-reading; claims linked to evidence; changed sources detected; **visible failure** when fidelity cannot be guaranteed. **No savings figure is asserted in advance** (CO-12 Telemetry-Before-Claims).

## 6. UKDL rules sealed by this corpus

- **`T-IIC-CIVILIZATION-DUPLICATION-001`** — A civilization that duplicates a sealed PP system (SQI, DRK, FD, ACIS, D2A, CO, GK, PM) without a genuine delta is architectural debt. The Wave-0 Reality Audit is the only mechanism that prevents it. **Observed: 22 candidates → 8 built; 22 of the source's 30 founding guardians already shipped.** Without STOP #1, this corpus would have produced ~500,000 words duplicating the 108,598 SQI already holds.
- **`PR-IIC-DATASET-FIRST-GOVERNS-001`** — DAIF follows the Dataset First Protocol: reality inspection → corpus architecture → dataset contracts → construction → integration → validation → *only then* implementation. The corpus builds the substrate that lets production systems be built correctly; it does not build them.
- **`T-IIC-ORGCHART-IS-NOT-MECHANISM-001`** — Naming a layer "Supreme Court", "Senate", "Immune System", or "Digital Twin" adds zero mechanism. A dataset ships only if it names an engine, a gate, and an evaluator. **Rejected under this rule: the CDT digital twin, the IGK org-chart, and the five-civilization framing.**
- **`PR-DAIF-D2A-PLUS-DEFAULT-001`** — Every candidate in a corpus build passes the D2A+ pipeline before construction; `CREATE_NEW` is the rarest verdict; the anti-inflation gate is applied to the proposal itself. **Origin: this map turned the Owner's own 22-candidate list into 8 by the Owner's own law.**

## 7. The fundamental property

> Institutional intelligence is not the accumulation of knowledge but the **maintenance of its correspondence with a world that will not hold still**. Every mechanism in these eight datasets defends one of the seven ways that correspondence silently breaks. And the discipline that produced the corpus is the one it teaches: **reinforce before you create, compose before you duplicate, and build a sovereign system only for the irreducible remainder** — because an institution that answers every new idea with a new system does not become more capable, it becomes less able to change, and a thing that cannot change cannot stay true.
