The Quality Benchmark Profile the re-spec §2 asks for **already exists** as the SQI fabrication contract
(`sqi/CANONICAL_ONTOLOGY.md` §9), itself extracted from these same CommonWealth/human-resonance references.

**1,200–1,500 words per Part.** A Part is rejected if repetitive · generic · padded · a summary not a
mechanism · missing data flows/failure modes/interfaces/governance/evaluation · disconnected from siblings ·
or re-narrating a parent instead of cross-referencing it.

~8,000 w/Part; SQI's own 25,782–28,596 words at 1,357 w/Part. **DAIF target: ~20 Parts × ~1,350 w = ~27k/dataset.**
