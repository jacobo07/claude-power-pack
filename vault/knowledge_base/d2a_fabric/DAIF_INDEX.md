## Epistemic marking (binding)

Every claim carries one of `EXISTS · PARTIALLY_EXISTS · SPECIFIED · PROPOSED · HYPOTHETICAL ·
REQUIRES_VALIDATION`. DAIF **specifies** a compilation substrate; it does not **claim** one runs.
No benchmark/savings/ROI/precision figure is asserted unmeasured (CO-12 Telemetry-Before-Claims).
