# CPCSC — DECISION LOG

## D-001 · 2026-07-20 · STOP #1 ruling (Owner)

**Decision:** Option A — fold CPCSC into the open CPP-ACI STOP #1 (one estate, one gate) —
**plus** apply Duplicate-to-Advantage so that detected duplicates also *reinforce* their existing
owners, and genuinely-absent datasets still get built.

**Rationale carried:** two independent instruments (the 2026-07-12 CPP-ACI Tribunal and this
2026-07-20 CPCSC audit, different source documents, different denominators) returned the same
shape: ~0–1 clean NEW domains against a sealed estate. A second parallel STOP gate on one estate
is duplication at the governance layer.

**Consequences:**
1. CPCSC does **not** become a 13th sibling family. It becomes an input to the CPP-ACI mission —
   its unique findings merge into that ledger.
2. Every ABSORB/REJECT verdict now carries a **D2A obligation**: the duplicate must be tested as
   a possible *advantage* for the existing owner (missing layer, missing engine, missing
   evaluation) before it is closed out. A REJECT that yields no reinforcement proposal is an
   incomplete verdict, not a finished one.
3. Genuinely-absent domains are still built. Confirmed candidate: Cognitive Education.
4. The CPP-IAS denominator gap (478k unversioned words) is escalated as an operational finding
   independent of the corpus question.

**Blocked on:** content-verification of the [T]-tier verdicts before the corpus boundary is
locked at STOP #2. Two verifiers dispatched 2026-07-20.

**Superseded:** nothing. **Expiry:** re-open if the CPP-ACI STOP #1 is itself rejected by the Owner.
