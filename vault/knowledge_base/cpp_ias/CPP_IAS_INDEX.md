and **omits `d2a_fabric` (DAIF) entirely — 299,397 words, the single largest family**,

tested. Any ABSORB/REFERENCE verdict citing this registry inherits the gap. Treat every
such verdict as title-tier under `T-OWNERSHIP-AUDIT-ABSORPTION-BIAS-001` until
re-verified at content tier.
