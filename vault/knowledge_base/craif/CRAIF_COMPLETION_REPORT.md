## Executive Summary

CRAIF began as a proposal to build a 16-family "Autonomous Integrity & Self-Healing Fabric"
(AISHF) corpus. A mandatory STOP #1 ownership audit, reading real mechanism rather than
titles, found ~75-80% of that proposal already owned by sealed Claude Power Pack corpus
(`modules/liveness/`, SQI, DRK, CPP-IAS D2, DAIF-00/21). Rather than build ~500,000 words of
duplicate governance, CRAIF was scoped to the irreducible residue — the transactional
execution of a repair, and the end-to-end simulation of whether a capability actually
activates — plus 8 proposals converting the 75-80% overlap into Duplicate-to-Advantage
reinforcement for the systems that already own it. The Reality Scan re-verification the
Owner mandated before Phase 0 architecture held the original estimate; no correction was
needed.
