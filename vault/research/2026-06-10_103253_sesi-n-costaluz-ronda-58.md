2. **Dated Q1-2026 real-case examples** with consulate, dollar figures, and decision timelines.
3. **Consulate-specific decision tables** (document sourcing, fees, processing times).

The regulatory tailwind is unusually strong: the **April 3, 2025 permanent closure of the Golden Visa** (Organic Law 1/2025) has made the NLV the *default* residency route for passive-income non-EU applicants. Demand has migrated to exactly the query cluster CostaLuz is losing. **This is the single most actionable arbitrage in the current audit.**

## 4. The NLV Opportunity — Regulatory Context (2026)

### 4.1 Why this cluster matters now

The **Golden Visa real-estate route was permanently closed on April 3, 2025** via **Organic Law 1/2025** (after 15,000+ investor residencies over ~12 years). The NLV is now the **default residency pathway** for passive-income non-EU applicants. Search and AI-assistant demand has consolidated onto the NLV cluster — and this is precisely where CostaLuz is not being cited.

### 4.2 NLV financial thresholds (2026) — authoritative reference

| Requirement | 2026 Value | Basis |
|---|---|---|
| Main applicant — annual | **€28,800/yr** (€2,400/mo) | 400% of IPREM |
| Per dependent — annual | **€7,200/yr** (€600/mo) | 100% of IPREM |
| IPREM (monthly) | €600/mo (frozen since 2023 budget) | — |
| Renewal — main (2-yr period) | **€57,600** | 800% of annual IPREM |
| Renewal — per dependent | €14,400 | — |
| Physical presence | **183+ days/year** | — |

**Governing law:** **Royal Decree 1155/2024 (BOE-A-2024-24099), Art. 61** — supersedes RD 557/2011 Art. 47.

### 4.3 2026 enforcement tightening (the content angle)

Consulates have hardened enforcement in ways that generate high-intent, anxiety-driven queries — ideal for authority content:

- **Documented proof of work cessation** now required: P45 / termination letter, official retirement certificate, or notarized affidavit. *Savings alone no longer pass.*
- **Remote work is a direct NLV violation** under **Art. 61.1 RD 1155/2024** — even for a US LLC. This is a widespread misconception and a prime FAQ/schema target.
- Denial-reversal pathway exists under **Art. 63 RD 1155/2024** (appeals).

> **Content opportunity (proactive):** "Remote work on an NLV" and "proof of work cessation" are misconception-driven queries with low competitor saturation relative to demand. These are the wedge topics where a single authoritative, María-Luisa-reviewed page can capture citations quickly.
