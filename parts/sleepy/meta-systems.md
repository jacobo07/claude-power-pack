# PART UMS — UNIVERSAL META-SYSTEMS (Sleepy)

Lazy-loaded on a meta-systems keyword or `/cpp-meta-systems`. Bridges the Power
Pack to the read-only corpus of seven **universal, domain-blind meta-systems**.
Full module at `modules/universal-meta-systems/` (start at its `SKILL.md`).

**Opt-in only.** This part is silent until triggered. A project that never
mentions the meta-systems never inherits them — they compose *with* a project's
own architecture, never over it.

## The seven (one line each)

- **MS-0 Provenance Substrate** — reproducibility-and-lineage by construction (floor).
- **MS-1 Composition Fabric** — assemble from published contracts.
- **MS-3 Compounding Substrate** — shared frontier; each build cheaper than the last.
- **MS-4 Capital Ledger** — book non-code capital as first-class assets.
- **MS-2 Intent Compiler** — intent → concern planes → contracts → assembled system.
- **MS-5 Evolution & Integrity Fabric** — evolve forever, safely, without drift.
- **MS-6 Absence Engine** — model what's missing/dormant; route it to be filled.

Loop: `MS-6 → MS-2 → MS-1 → MS-0 → MS-3 → MS-4 → MS-5 → MS-6`.

## Apply-protocol (domain-substitution — the one move)

1. State your intent/artifact in your own words.
2. Pick the meta-system by desired outcome (`index.md`).
3. Map your domain's nouns onto the dataset's abstract nouns.
4. Instantiate the dataset's laws verbatim against your map.
5. Universality check — meaningful & non-trivial under your map ⇒ correct.

## Hard rules (this part)

- Corpus is **read-only** — never edit `datasets/`/`phases/`/master files.
- **Universality preserved** — never hardcode a domain; nouns stay abstract.
- **Expose, don't reimplement** — hand over doctrine + noun-map; running code is
  a separate build in the consuming project.

## Return-to-dormant

After the meta-systems task completes, this part returns to dormant and reloads
only on the next trigger.
